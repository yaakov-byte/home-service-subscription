Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

1. Who We Are

This privacy policy applies to Home Service Subscription, operated by YY Business Solutions LLC, a Connecticut limited liability company located in Waterbury, CT. This policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, enroll in a membership, or interact with our support team.

2. Information We Collect

When you enroll in a membership, we collect your first and last name, email address, phone number, and the city or region where you need service. We do not collect, store, or have access to your full credit card number, expiration date, or CVV. Payment card data is entered directly into a secure, PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment form. We receive only a non-reversible token representing your payment method, the last four digits of your card, and the card brand (e.g., Visa, Mastercard) for your reference.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information to process your membership enrollment, send billing receipts and renewal notices, coordinate dispatch and scheduling with contractors in your area, communicate with you about your account or service requests, and respond to support inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Information Shared with Third Parties

We share limited information with the following categories of service providers, solely to operate the membership and deliver service. Payment processing: your tokenized payment method is transmitted to our payment processor to process charges. Our payment processor does not share your card data with us. Contractor dispatch: when you request a service call, we share your name, phone number, and service address with the assigned contractor so they can schedule and complete your repair. We do not share your payment information with contractors. Communications: we may use email or SMS service providers to send you receipts, renewal notices, and support correspondence.

5. Call Recordings

Phone calls with our team may be recorded for quality assurance and to document your enrollment consent. Call recordings that capture your verbal agreement to the membership terms are retained as proof of authorization. We do not record credit card numbers — if you provide payment details by phone, our agents direct you to a secure online payment link rather than accepting card numbers verbally. Call recordings are stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel for billing verification and dispute resolution purposes only.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and transaction records for as long as your membership is active and for a period of seven years following cancellation, as required for tax, accounting, and dispute resolution purposes. Call recordings documenting enrollment consent are retained for the same period. You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us, subject to our legal retention obligations.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) on all pages of our website, PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment handling, role-based access controls for internal staff, and secure storage with access logging for all customer records. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate information, request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements), opt out of certain data sharing, and receive a copy of your data in a portable format. California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@homeservicesubscription.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses only essential cookies required for the payment form to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that follow you across other websites. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated and you will be notified.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has enrolled, contact us and we will promptly delete the account and process a refund.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email at least 30 days before they take effect. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, contact YY Business Solutions LLC at support@homeservicesubscription.com, or write to us at YY Business Solutions LLC, Waterbury, CT.
