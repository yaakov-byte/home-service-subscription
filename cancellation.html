Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective date: June 2026

Cancel anytime, online

You can cancel your membership instantly — no phone call required, no waiting on hold, no hoops. Use any of the methods below and your cancellation takes effect immediately.

How to Cancel

You can cancel your Home Service Subscription membership through any of the following methods. Online: email support@homeservicesubscription.com with the subject line "Cancel Membership" and include your name and the email address on your account. You will receive a cancellation confirmation within one business day. By phone: call our support line during business hours and request cancellation. The agent will process it while you're on the line. In writing: mail a cancellation request to YY Business Solutions LLC, Waterbury, CT. Cancellation is effective upon receipt.

What Happens When You Cancel

When you cancel, all future renewal charges stop immediately. You will not be billed again. Your membership benefits — including priority scheduling and dispatch access — remain active through the end of your current paid annual period. For example, if you enrolled on March 15 and cancel on August 10, you retain full membership access through the following March 14. No partial-year fees or early termination penalties apply.

Refund Policy

If you cancel within 30 days of your initial enrollment or within 30 days of any annual renewal charge, you are entitled to a full refund of that charge, no questions asked. To request a refund, contact us by email, phone, or mail using the methods listed above and state that you are requesting a refund. Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5 to 10 business days after approval.

After the 30-day window, the membership fee for the current annual period is non-refundable. However, if you experience a service failure — for example, a contractor does not arrive for a scheduled appointment, or we are unable to dispatch a contractor in your area — contact our support team. We review service-failure refund requests on a case-by-case basis and will issue a full or partial refund where we determine that we did not deliver the service you paid for.

Renewal Notices

We will send you an email reminder at least 30 days before your annual renewal date, notifying you of the upcoming charge and reminding you how to cancel if you choose not to renew. If we do not have a valid email address on file, we will attempt to reach you by phone or text. If we cannot notify you in advance, and your card is charged at renewal, you are entitled to a full refund of that renewal charge upon request regardless of the 30-day window.

Disputed Charges

If you see a charge from HOME SERVICE SUBSCR on your statement that you don't recognize or believe is an error, please contact us first at support@homeservicesubscription.com before contacting your bank. We can typically resolve billing questions and issue refunds faster than a bank dispute process, and reaching out to us directly helps us serve you better. We aim to respond to all billing inquiries within one business day.
